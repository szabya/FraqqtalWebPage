About Us

Fraqqtal provides consulting services and specializes in the development, validation, implementation, and distribution of software solutions and related devices and IT systems. Our focus areas include quantitative modeling, quantum computation, machine learning, and artificial intelligence, along with their applications across various domains. We also conduct research on these technologies and perform scientific studies on behalf of clients or in our own interest. Our company supports and carries out certification procedures, regulatory approvals, and patent applications. We can establish branches and subsidiaries both domestically and internationally, participate in other companies, and engage in all transactions directly or indirectly related to our purpose. We are also able to acquire, encumber, sell, and manage real estate properties, provided that legal requirements are met. Additionally, we offer financing for our own or third-party accounts and provide guarantees and sureties for subsidiaries and third parties.
